How to Use AI Podcast Generator

Creating a podcast is simple:

  1. Enter your desired topic in detail
  2. Set the number of participants and conversation rounds
  3. Configure each character with unique backgrounds and personalities
  4. Click "Generate Podcast" and wait for the magic to happen

Good character configuration creates engaging podcasts:

  • Name: Give each character a memorable name
  • Gender: Affects voice selection
  • Personality: Determines speaking style and tone
  • Background: The most important field - describe expertise, experience, and perspective
  • Voice Preference: Optional voice characteristics

  • Be specific about your topic and desired discussion points
  • Create characters with complementary perspectives
  • Use detailed backgrounds that give characters expertise in different areas
  • Standard conversation rounds (8) work well for most topics
  • Allow 2-5 minutes for generation depending on complexity
